Hi there, I'm Pacman, you know me, right?
I'm here to help you. if you don't need me, you can click anywhere outside of this box to close this. I'll be here for you at the bottom left corner. But if you need my help, click "Next" so I can show you my tricks!
OR you can see this YouTube Video on how to play the game

You can choose to play with computer or your firend in the same computer. If you choose to play with computer, you can select the difficulty level of the game (no one has won the hard level so far!) Then you can start the game by "Serving" the ball. Which is clicking left/right arrow. Whoever score 7 first will win the game.

If you choose to play with your friend, player one should user the arrow keys, and player2 should use the AWSD keys. Whoever score 7 first will win the game.
